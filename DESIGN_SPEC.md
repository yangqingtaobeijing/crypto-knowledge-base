# Crypto Knowledge Base Design Spec

## Visual Direction: Brutalist Experimental (Blue/Gold Variant)
- **Philosophy**: Bold typography, visible grids, high contrast, avoiding "AI dark mode" (pure black).
- **Palette (OKLch)**:
  - `--bg`: oklch(95% 0.02 240) (Light tinted background)
  - `--surface`: oklch(100% 0 0) (Pure white surfaces)
  - `--fg`: oklch(20% 0.06 245) (Deep Space Blue - the "dark" anchor)
  - `--border`: oklch(20% 0.06 245) (1.5px - 2px thick borders)
  - `--accent`: oklch(75% 0.15 85) (Bitcoin Gold)
  - `--muted`: oklch(45% 0.04 245)

## Pages & Navigation
1. **Index (Launcher)**: Cards for each section.
2. **Landing**: "The Crypto Primer" - Hero, mission, teaser.
3. **History**: Vertical timeline of decentralization.
4. **Coins**: Detailed grid of BTC, ETH, SOL, etc.
5. **Literature**: Minimalist table of whitepapers and research links.

## Interaction
- Large hover states.
- Underlined links.
- No rounded corners (0px - 2px max).
